Green Traveller

We are here to help you have an oversight on what you're travelling with and how much CO2 is produced by that. With this application you can set your own goals or limits on how much you want to produce per month und keep track of how much you have already used. With a better understanding of your own behaviour you can already make a change for the better.
This application is made for you, so all the data will be stored locally on your device.
Click on the Button below and you will be guided through a small initital setup.
